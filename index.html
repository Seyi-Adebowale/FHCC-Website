WHY CHOOSE US?

14-hour Service

Our services are very favourable for working Parents. Our services commence at 6am and end at 8pm

365-Days Availability

No Terminal Holidays for the Pre-Schoolers and Special Services on Weekends & Public Holidays at specialized rates means we are available every day of the year.

Learning and fun

We have Kids' Library and Kids' Practical Workshop to provide the kids with hands-on activities that will make learning very enjoyable for them.

Naturally Nurtured Playground

Our outdoor playground is tailored to enable kids interact creatively with nature and the environment.

Positive Learning Environment

Our environment is very clean, friendly, conducive and secure for learning. Each child is given personal attention in a small-class setting.

Healthy Meals

We provide healthy meals as an healthy diet is very important for a child's general well-being.

High Staff-to-Child Ratio

We have a high staff-to-child ratio (3:1) that guarantees that the kids receive adequate care, attention and supervision

24/7 Surveillance

Surveillance cameras are installed and functioning 24/7 to provide a heightened sense of security.
