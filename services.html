CRECHE

We provide Day Care services for babies, toddlers and young children. We provide a loving, secure atmosphere where infants get exceptional care and plenty of affection to support their development

Our team of dedicated care-givers are deeply committed to giving the children the best care needed for their development.

PRE-NURSERY

At the age of 24 months, the kids begin pre-nursery class. They will start to identify surrounding objects and pictures, and develop awareness of their preferences and dislikes

At this stage, the kids will start to connect with their peer group, learn how to share and sort toys, and develop friendships. We help them to develop their language and speaking skills through rhymes, stories and social interaction.

NURSERY & KINDERGARTEN

At this stage, the kids respond well to staff expectations, display care for others and have the capacity to communicate their thoughts and ideas to adults. They also begin to make independent decisions during activities.

We help them to develop the essential literacy skills, numerical skills, social skills, cognitive skills and physical skills in preparation for Primary Level Education.

AFTER SCHOOL CARE

We provide After-School services for kids after the close of the regular school day during the academic year. Our After-School services run from 2pm to 8pm daily.

Perks of our after-school care include:
**Assistance with school assignments
**Tutoring
**Naps, Bath and Change of clothes
**Various fun learning activities

WEEKEND CARE

We provide child care services on weekends and public holidays. We are aware that circumstances could make Parents require child care needs beyond the typical workweek hours of Monday through Friday

However, such services will require a pre-booking at specialized rates. Check out our rates for more information on pricing.

HOSTEL

We provide hostel services for kids whose Parents are out of town. We provide meals during the period of hostelling. This service is available ONLY by pre-booking

It is advised that the pre-booking is done early enough so that the necessary planning can be concluded before the commencement of the hostelling period.
